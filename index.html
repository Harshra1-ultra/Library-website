Introduction

Create the Perfect Environment For Your Study Success

Distractions are the biggest hurdle in preparing for UPSC, JEE, NEET, banking, and civil services. We offer a quiet study space equipped with personal charging points, high-speed fiber internet, and comfortable ergonomics at Gaya-Fatehpur highway, Khatangi Kothi.

Each student gets a personal study seat. Our study hall is fully air-conditioned and monitored continuously via CCTV security systems to maintain a silent environment.

Important Rules

Library Rules & Regulations

1. Maintain Complete Silence inside the reading hall. Discussions, calls, or group talks must take place outside.

2. Keep mobile phones in Silent Mode at all times. Use headphones when playing reference videos or lectures.

3. CCTV cameras are active. Strict action will be taken in case of any misconduct.
